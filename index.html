About College

Anurag Engineering College is established in 2001 and is reputed as one of the most preferred colleges for engineering education within short time. The college has created a resourceful and inspiring academic ambience and world-class infrastructure.

The efforts have borne fruit and today Anurag students have access to the best learning environment to transform them into competent engineers. The overall pass percentage in more than 95% as on today. Most of the Anurag students are well settled either in job in some of the best MNCs or pursuing their Post Graduation in IITs, IIITs, NITs and foreign universities.
